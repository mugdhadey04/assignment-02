Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten offers an holistic and nurturing, Early Years Education for ages 3 to 6, covering the ages of pre-school, nursery, Reception and Year 1.

Elementary School

Basic subjects are taught in elementary school, and students often remain in one classroom throughout the school day (until starting different blocks), except for physical education, library, music, and art classes.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school, also known as intermediate school, junior high school, junior secondary school, or lower secondary school, is an educational stage between primary school and secondary school.
